FAQ

Help!

Are these meals good for beginners?

While there aspects of some of these dishes that may be difficult, for the most part they are for beginners! We wanted to make sure we made each dish as easy to accomplish as possible so everyone can enjoy these delicious meals.

Will you do food from (insert anime) next!?

Yes! Our dream is to have every dish from every anime, show, or game possible, but that's a little hard so we love to hear what others want to see from us! Keep sending us recommendations and maybe you'll see your dish soon.

Where can I find some of these ingredients?

A lot of the ingredients you should be able to find at your local grocery store. If you can't find something there, you can always try asian markets such as H-mart.

Are these accurate to the movies?

While they aren't perfect, we tried our best to make them as accurate as they could be while still maintaining easily accessible ingredients.

I'm confused about (insert step)... Help!

Never fear! We understand that sometimes a step or term can be confusing. A lot of the time youtube has helpful videos on how to do certain cooking practices. If you aren't able to find a video or tutorial online, send us a message and we will guide you how to do a certain step!

Are these child safe recipes?

While these recipes are perfect for beginners we do recommend that a child has parental guidence when cooking any of these dishes. This is because a lot of the dishes do involve using knives and stove/oven. With that said, we do plan to add more child safe ones in the future!
