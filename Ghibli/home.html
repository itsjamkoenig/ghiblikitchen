Ghibli's
Kitchen

Have you always wanted to make the delicious food from your favorite Ghibli movies? Well, now's your chance! With the recipes in this cookbook, you can prepare meals just like those characters in your favorite beautiful animated films. The recipes are laid out and easy to follow, so you can enjoy Japanese dishes that might just rival the fare at Kokuriko-Zaka!
